Taskbar

View as standalone SVG

The toggleOn() and toggleOff() methods allow to show or hide the pergola.taskbar system object. Its position can get the value "top" (default) or "bottom". You can also override its initial display value.

The pergola.taskbar carries window tabs and previews. You can see them in any window example.
