Libraries

Pergola libraries are of three different categories: engines for computer assisted production, preset definitions, and repositories of strings. Some are simple and straightforward to use, and some imply a learning curve, hopefully providing excitement and fun in exchange. In-depth information in the Libraries chapter in the tutorial.
